Our story

Food Website was born out of a love for culinary artistry and a desire to bring people together over delicious food. Our journey began in 2024 when Chef Mohamed decided to share their culinary expertise with the world. Since then, we’ve been on a mission to delight taste buds and create lasting memories... Our inviting, comfortable dining room is the perfect place to enjoy a great meal. Or, grab a seat at our neighborhood pub-like bar—our community tables provide an energetic and lively atmosphere that is perfect for our famous Thursday Night Trivia.The Restaurant is proud to serve more than 20 million guests a year in over 90 restaurants throughout the Northeast. We will continue to prosper and grow and will continue to take care of every guest who walks through our doors, just like we’ve always done. We treat people right, and that’s what makes new guests feel welcome, and regulars feel right at home.
